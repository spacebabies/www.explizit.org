Explizit song archive

A great variety of tracked music groups have Web pages. The most famous of these groups is probably Kosmic [...]. Other favorites include [...] Dutch dance divas Explizit, who produce the kind of electronic music you have to pay money for to own on music cd. - PCFormat Aug '96.

Listed below are all songs ever produced by Explizit. First releases at the top, last releases at the bottom. Note that these songs are in module (MOD/S3M/IT/XM) format and can only be played by so-called Module Players. They're not MP3 or WAV files.

Download all songs in one big ZIP (60 MB). Thanks to very kind donations from long-time fans all tracks have been recovered. I will do my best to store them here indefinitely. If you still have any missing songs or other Explizit material you want to share, please email me at joost [at] spacebabies.nl.
